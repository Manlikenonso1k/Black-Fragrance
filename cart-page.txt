**Prompt:**
Create a high-fidelity, professional UI design for an e-commerce shopping cart page. 

**Visual Style:**
* Palette: Monochrome (Strictly black, white, and varying shades of grey).
* Typography: High-end Serif for titles; clean, modern Sans-Serif for functional text and numbers.
* Aesthetics: Minimalist, spacious, 1px borders, and low-radius corners (2-4px). 

**Layout & Components:**
* Navigation & Progress: A top header with text links and utility icons. Include a subtle three-step checkout progress indicator (Cart, Shipping, Payment) using thin lines and numbered circles.
* Cart List (Left Column): 
    * Individual product rows featuring a square thumbnail image, product title, variant details (e.g., size/scent), and price.
    * Interactive elements: A compact quantity stepper (- 1 +), a text-link "Remove" button, and a "Save for later" checkbox.
    * Promo Code Section: A clean, wide input field with a ghost "Apply" button located below the item list.
* Order Summary (Right Sidebar): 
    * A sticky container with a light grey background.
    * Line items for Subtotal, Discounts, and Estimated Taxes.
    * A mini-form for "Shipping Estimate" with simple input fields for ZIP and Country.
    * A prominent, solid black "Proceed to Checkout" call-to-action button.
* Upsell Section: A "You might also like" horizontal grid at the bottom featuring product cards with a "Quick Add" button.
* Footer: A multi-column layout for brand info, navigation links, and a newsletter subscription box.

**Technical Details:**
* Use high contrast for primary buttons.
* Maintain generous whitespace between sections to ensure a clean, premium feel.
* Ensure all dividers are light grey and 1px thick.
